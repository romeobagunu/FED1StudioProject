Class Subjects

History

The origin of the "MOBA" genre is explored, with particular respect to the evolution of DOTA 2.

Mechanics

The fundamental variables which affect gameplay are defined and studied.

Combat

The principles of PvP combat with respect to roles, lanes, team fighting, and other topics.

Economy

The principles of farming, split pushing, and other economic considerations.

Strategy

The principles of drafting, win conditions, and playstyles.

Communication

An exploration of team dynamics, mentality, and psychology.
