About Us

DOTA 2 can be one of the most difficult games to learn - and perhaps even harder to master. ALPHA is here to help. With a dynamic curriculum and veteran instructors, we'll teach you everything you need to know to get out of the trenches and emerge into the highest levels of ranked play. If you're up for the challenge, we'll take anyone from zero to hero.

Why Choose Us

A Dynamic Curriculum

DOTA 2 is a game that is constantly changing. With every update comes the addition of new heroes, the introduction of new mechanics, and the inevitable nerfing and buffing of heroes. Each one of these factors results in a fast-paced meta that can be vastly different from year-to-year. While our introductory and intermediate content remains relatively stable, our advanced courses are always evolving to keep you up to speed with the latest developments in the ranked and professional metagame.
